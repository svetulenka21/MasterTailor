Master Tailor is an expert alterations and clothing repair shop located in Northallerton, North Yorkshire.

We provide our customers with a wide range of garment alterations services and repairs including; tailoring, zippers, custom fittings, button replacement, leather alterations, handbags, jackets, dress hemming, skirts, trousers adjustments and curtains.

Alongside this, we also offer comprehensive dry cleaning, laundry and ironing services to all of our clients.

Whether you are looking for a special occasion piece or something casual we are able to do it for you. We take pride in delivering high quality service, products while delivering amazing customer service. Come stop in our shop and take a look at some of our bespoke work.
